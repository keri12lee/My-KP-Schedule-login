# Contribution Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the My KP Schedule Login portal repository! We welcome developers, designers, and testers of all skill levels to help improve this platform. 

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by our terms and maintain a welcoming, collaborative environment.

---

We follow a standard fork-and-pull workflow to manage changes:

1. **Fork the Repository:** Create your own copy of this project by clicking the "Fork" button at the top of this page.
2. **Clone Your Fork:** Bring the code to your local machine:
